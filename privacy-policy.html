Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Happy Wedding, an offline wedding planning application developed and published by Biswa Bangla Social Networking Club. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

By using Happy Wedding, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Happy Wedding is a fully offline application. All data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

The information you may enter includes:

  • Your name and login credentials (stored locally on your device)
  • Guest list details (names, contact numbers)
  • Catering preferences and vendor information
  • Wedding venue, hotel, and location details
  • Budget and checklist items
  • Photography and event planning notes

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of this data.

3. No Internet or Backend Connection

Happy Wedding does not require an internet connection to function. There is no backend server, no database, and no cloud storage connected to this application. All functionality is entirely local to your device.

4. User Account (Local Only)

The app allows you to manually sign up and log in. These credentials are stored only on your device using local device storage (Room Database). They are not synced, backed up, or accessible by us or any third party.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties because we do not collect any user data in the first place.

This app does not use:

  • Analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media integrations

6. Permissions

Happy Wedding may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage: To save and read your wedding planning data locally

No other sensitive permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts) are accessed without your explicit action.

7. Children's Privacy

Happy Wedding is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secure and not sharing your login credentials with others.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data held by us to access, correct, or delete. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing app data from your device settings.
